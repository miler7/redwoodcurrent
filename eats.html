Submit Your Restaurant News

Use this form to share what's happening at your restaurant this week. Your submission will appear in Thursday's Redwood Current newsletter, which goes out to subscribers across Redwood Falls, Belview, Morton, and Lower Sioux Community.

Deadline: Wednesday at 6:00 PM

Character limit: 170 characters (about 25-30 words)

What to share: New menu items, weekly specials, limited-time offerings, events at your restaurant, hour changes, or other timely news.

Have a question? Email justin@redwoodcurrent.com

Tips for great submissions:

  • Be specific and timely (not generic advertising)
  • Change your content week to week to keep it fresh
  • Include relevant details (dates, times, prices if applicable)
  • Write clearly—we may edit for length or clarity

Current Eats Guidelines

Editorial Policy

  • We reserve the right to edit your submission for length, content, formatting, spelling, or any other reason
  • We may not contact you about edits
  • We may exclude content that doesn't meet our standards or is repetitive week-to-week
  • If we have questions and you don't respond in time, we may not include your submission

Listing Order

Restaurants are listed in the order submissions are received (first submitted, first listed).

No Exceptions

  • Late submissions will not be accepted
  • We do not send reminder emails
  • The Wednesday 6:00 PM deadline is firm

What We Don't Include

  • Generic promotional taglines or descriptions
  • The same content repeated week after week
  • Political or controversial content
  • Non-restaurant-related information

Program Details

Current Eats is free at least through February 2025. We may introduce paid advertising options in the future or discontinue the program at any time. Participating restaurants would not be automatically enrolled in any paid program—we would invite you to opt in at that time.
